### Grammar Review: Sandhi Rules (Vowels)

## Exercise

A) Apply vowel Sandhi rules to these word pairs:
1. *tatra + asti* » *tatrāsti* (there is)
2. *guruḥ + iti* » *gururiti* (thus the teacher)
3. *bālā + api* » *bālāpi* (the girl also)
4. *yaḥ + icchati* » *yocchati* (who desires)

B) Write the following sentences without and with Sandhi:
1. *Sā atra asti.* » *Sātrāsti.* (She is here.)
2. *Nṛpaḥ uktvān.* » *Nṛpauktvān.* (The king spoke.)
