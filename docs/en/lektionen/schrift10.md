# Script Exercise 10

### Ligatures (Samyuktākṣara) - Part 3: Rule 2

[See [Script Exercise 8](schrift08.md) for Rule 1]

If the first consonant of a ligature does not end in a vertical bar (is "rounded" at the top or bottom), the following consonant is placed **under** the first, often losing its top horizontal bar.

#### Exceptions: ma and ya
If **ma** (म्) or **ya** (य्) are the second member, they are written in a shortened form behind the first character.
*   **kma**: क्म, **ṅma**: ङ्म, **dma**: द्म, **hma**: ह्म
*   **kya**: क्य, **chya**: छ्य, **dya**: द्य, **hya**: ह्य

#### Important Common Ligatures:
*   **kta**: क्त, **tta**: त्त, **dda**: द्द, **ddha**: द्ध, **dna**: द्न, **dbha**: द्भ, **hna**: ह्न, **dva**: द्व, **hva**: ह्व
*   **kṣa**: क्ष, **jña**: ज्ञ, **ṇṇa**: ण्ण, **hṇa**: ह्ण
