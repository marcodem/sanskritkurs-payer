# Lesson 17

## 17.1. Second Present Class (अदादि = अद् etc.)

The verbal endings are attached directly to the root. In this process, the phonetic alterations resulting from word-sandhi must be taken into account.

The following formation patterns occur in the second present class:

1.  Roots with stem gradation 
    *   mostly: strong grade – weak grade
    *   certain roots: lengthened grade – weak grade 
2.  Roots without stem gradation 
3.  Two-syllable roots ending in -i/-ī with stem gradation

## 17.2. Roots of the second present class with stem gradation: Strong grade – Weak grade

Examples:

द्विष् 2 U "to hate"

*   strong present stem: dveṣ-
*   weak present stem: dviṣ-

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | द्वेष्टि (« dveṣ + -ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | द्विषन्ति (dviṣ-anti) |
> | 3rd sg. Pres. Ā | द्विष्टे (« dviṣ + -te) |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. Ā | द्विषते (dviṣ-ate) |

The following word-sandhi rule applies here:

Word-sandhi of -ṣ + t(h)-

\-ṣ + t(h)- » -ṣṭ(h)-

Further examples:

इ 2 P "to go" (Ātmanepada after certain prefixes)

*   strong present stem: e-
*   weak present stem:
    *   before a consonant: i-
    *   before a vowel: y-/iy-

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | एति (e-ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | यन्ति (y-anti) |
> | 3rd sg. Pres. Ā | इते (i-te) |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. Ā | इयते (iy-ate) |

दुह् 2 U "to milk"

*   strong present stem: doh-
*   weak present stem: duh-

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | दोग्धि (« doh- + -ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | दुहन्ति (duh-anti) |
> | 3rd sg. Pres. Ā | दुग्धे (« duh- + -te) |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. Ā | दुहते (duh-ate) |

हन् 2 P "to strike, to slay, to kill"

*   strong present stem: han-
*   weak present stem:
    *   before a consonant: ha- (« \*hn-)
    *   before a vowel: ghn-/han-

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | हन्ति (han-ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | घ्नन्ति (ghn-anti) |
> | 3rd sg. Pres. Ā | (हते) (ha-te) |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. Ā | (घ्नते) (ghn-ate) |

अस् 2 P "to be"

*   strong present stem: as-
*   weak present stem: s-

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | अस्ति  (as-ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | सन्ति (s-anti) |

## 17.3. Roots of the second present class with stem gradation: Lengthening grade – Shortening grade

Example:

स्तु 2 U "to praise"

*   strong present stem: stau-
*   weak present stem:
    *   before a consonant: stu-
    *   before a vowel: stuv-

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | स्तौति (stau-ti)
> or: स्तवीति see under 5.
> 
>  |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | स्तुवन्ति (stuv-anti) |
> | 3rd sg. Pres. Ā | स्तुते (stu-te) |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. Ā | स्तुवते (stuv-ate) |

## 17.4. Roots of the Second Present Class without Stem Gradation

Roots of the second present class ending in -ā do not undergo stem gradation.

Example:

पा 2 P "to protect, to guard, to watch over"

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | पाति (pā-ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | पान्ति (« pā- + -anti) |

Certain other roots of the second class also lack stem gradation:

Examples:

अद् 2 P "to eat"

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | अत्ति (« ad- + -ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | अदन्ति (ad-anti) |

आस् 2 Ā "to sit"

> | 3rd sg. Pres. Ā | आस्ते (ās-te) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. Ā | आसते (ās-ate) |

वच् 2 P "to say"

> | 3rd sg. Pres. P | वक्ति (« vac- + -ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3rd pl. Pres. P | does not occur |

## 17.5. Two-Syllable Roots in -i/-ī with Stem Gradation

Several roots appear as two syllables in a series of forms, i.e., they possess an -i (or, before certain endings, an -ī) before a consonantal suffix. Nevertheless, these roots are nonetheless categorized as monosyllabic by native grammarians and in dictionaries, etc.

Examples:

रुद् 2 P "to weep, to wail"

*   strong present stem: rodi-
*   weak present stem:
    *   before a consonant: rudi-
    *   before a vowel: rud-

> | 3. sg. Pres. P | रोदिति (rodi-ti) |
> | --- | --- |
> | 3. pl. Pres. P | रुदन्ति (rud-anti) |

ब्रू 2 U "to speak"

*   strong present stem: bravī- (« bro- + -ī)
*   weak present stem:
    *   before a consonant: brū-
    *   before a vowel: bruv-

> | 3. sg. Pres. P | ब्रवीति (bravī-ti) |
    | --- | --- |
> | 3. pl. Pres. P | ब्रुवन्ति (bruv-anti) |
> | 3. sg. Pres. Ā | ब्रूते (brū-te) |
> | 
> 3. pl. Pres. Ā
> 
>  | ब्रुवते (bruv-ate) |

Similarly, स्तु 2 U "to praise" possesses, in addition to the forms specified under 3, forms following this pattern:

> | 3. sg. Pres. P | स्तवीति (« sto + ī + ti)
> or: स्तौति see under 3.
> 
>  |
> | --- | --- |
