# Exercise 8

### Grammar Review: Passive Construction (Karmaṇi)

## Exercise

A) Convert the following sentences from Active (Kartari) to Passive (Karmaṇi):
1. *Naraḥ phalaṃ khādati.* » *Narena phalaṃ khādyate.* (The fruit is eaten by the man.)
2. *Guruḥ śiṣyaṃ pṛcchati.* » *Gurunā śiṣyaḥ pṛcchyate.* (The student is asked by the teacher.)
3. *Gajāḥ vanaṃ gacchanti.* » *Gajaiḥ vanaṃ gamyate.* (The forest is gone to by the elephants.)

B) Translate into English:
1. *Kavinā devatā dṛśyate.* = The deity is seen by the poet.
2. *Rāmeṇa vanaṃ gamyate.* = The forest is being traveled to by Rāma.
3. *Taiḥ phalaṃ khādyate.* = The fruit is eaten by them.
