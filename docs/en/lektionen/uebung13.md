# Exercise 13

## Exercise

A) Translate the following sentences and convert them into Active Present (Lat) sentences:

1. अग्निना गृहं दग्धम् ।
   A fire has burned the house.
   (Active: अग्निर्गृहं दहति । The fire burns the house.)
2. बुद्धेन सत्यं बुद्धम् ।
   Buddha has awakened to the truth. / Buddha has recognized the truth.
   (Active: बुद्धः सत्यं बुध्यते / बोधति । Buddha recognizes the truth.)
3. बोध्या गौतमो मुक्तः ।
   Through enlightenment, Gautama was liberated.
   (Active: बोधिर्गौतमं मुञ्चति । Enlightenment liberates Gautama.)
4. शूद्रा मूढाः ।
   The Śūdras (or Śūdra women) are deluded.
   (Active: शूद्रा मुह्यन्ति । The Śūdras are deluded.)
5. ब्राह्मणेन मोक्ष इष्टः ।
   The Brahmin desired liberation.
   (Active: ब्राह्मणो मोक्षमिच्छति । The Brahmin desires liberation.)
6. रामेण पुण्यं कृतम् ।
   Rāma performed a meritorious deed.
   (Active: रामः पुण्यं करोति । Rāma performs a meritorious deed.)

B) Translate and convert into Passive Past (PPP) constructions:

1. राम इष्टमपि मोक्षं न लभते ।
   Even though Rāma desires liberation, he does not obtain it.
   (Passive: रामेणेष्टो ऽपि मोक्षो न लब्धः ।)
2. यज्ञो न मुञ्चति ।
   Sacrifice does not liberate.
   (Passive: यज्ञेन न मुक्तः ।)
3. साधवो देवान्स्मरन्ति ।
   The holy men remember the gods.
   (Passive: साधुभिर्देवाः स्मृताः ।)
4. पुण्यवान्पुत्रो देवान्यजते ।
   The virtuous son sacrifices to the gods.
   (Passive: पुण्यवता पुत्रेण देवा इष्टाः ।)
