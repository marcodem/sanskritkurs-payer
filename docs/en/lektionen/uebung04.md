# Exercise 4

### Grammar Review: Instrumental and Dative Cases

## Exercise

A) Translate into English:
1. *Naraḥ phalaṃ hastena khādati.* = The man eats the fruit with [his] hand.
2. *Guruḥ śiṣyāya pustakaṃ dadāti.* = The teacher gives a book to the student.
3. *Gajāḥ pādaiḥ gacchanti.* = The elephants go with [their] feet.

B) Translate into Sanskrit:
1. He goes to the city by chariot (Instrumental: *rathena*).
2. The father gives food to the son (Dative: *putrāya*).
3. We see the forest with [our] eyes (Instrumental plural: *netraiḥ*).

C) Form the Instrumental and Dative (Singular and Plural) for:
*   *nara*
*   *phala*
