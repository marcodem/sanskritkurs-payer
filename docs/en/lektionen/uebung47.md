# Exercise 47

A) Translate:

यदि गच्छसि गच्छ त्वम् । अहं न गमिष्यामि ॥१॥

> If you want to go, then go! I will not go.

आर्य प्रेक्षस्व मे परिभवम् ॥२॥

> Noble one, behold my humiliation!

भो राम यदि मया गन्तव्यं तदैषा कन्यापि मम सहायिनी भवतु ॥३॥

> O Rāma, if I must go, then let this girl also be my companion.

आर्ये तिष्ठ तिष्ठ । न त्वया भेतव्यम् ॥४॥

> Noble lady, stay, stay! You must not fear.

प्रसीदत्वार्यः ॥५॥

> May the noble one be pleased (calm down)!

आर्ये स्वागतं ते ॥६॥

> Welcome, noble lady.

आज्ञापयत्वार्यः किं मया क्रियतामिति ॥७॥

> Noble sir, please command what I should do.

युद्धाय युज्यस्व नैवं पापमवाप्स्यसि ॥८॥

> Prepare yourself for battle; thus you will not incur sin.

प्रश्नः : कस्मात्त्वं भीतः । प्रतिवचनम् : तस्य रामस्य गुणेभ्यः । प्रष्टा : के तस्य गुणा यस्य गृहं प्रविश्याशितव्यमपि नास्ति ॥९॥

> Question: Why are you afraid? Answer: Because of the qualities of that Rāma. Questioner: What are the qualities of one in whose house, when entered, there is not even anything to eat?

तवैव हस्ते शस्त्रं तिष्ठतु ॥१०॥

> Let the weapon stay in your hand alone!

भवति न ते परिभवस्तत्रभवतो रामस्य निवेदयितव्यः ॥११॥

> My lady, your humiliation should not be reported to the honorable Rāma.

B) Translate into Sanskrit (use the imperative):

1. I want to be supported/maintained.

> भ्रियै ॥१॥

2. He should be satisfied.

> तुष्यतु ॥२॥

3. We want to call.

> ह्वयाम ॥३॥

4. I want to bow to you (dative).

> तुभ्यं नमानि । नमानि ते ॥४॥

5. This deed should be done.

> एतत्कर्म क्रियताम् ॥५॥

6. You should sleep (passive construction).

> भवता सुप्यताम् ॥६॥

7. My son, strive for wealth!

> पुत्र धने / धनं / धनाय यतस्व ॥७॥

8. Begin the study!

> अध्ययनमारभध्वम् ॥८॥

9. Give (pra-yam) the rice-balls to the ancestors!

> पितृभ्यः पिण्डान्प्रयच्छत ॥९॥

10. They should rejoice!

> नन्दन्तु ॥१०॥

11. I want to look down upon the world.

> लोकमवेक्षै ॥११॥

12. We want to go (pad) to Benares.

> काशीं पद्यामहै ॥१२॥

13. They (pl.) should worship the gods with a sacrifice as masters of the sacrifice.

> देवान्यजन्ताम् ॥१३॥

14. Sing a song of praise!

> स्तोत्रं गाय ॥१४॥

15. Sons should be born to me!

> पुत्रा मह्यं / मे जायन्ताम् ॥१५॥

16. I want to show you my house.

> तुभ्यं मम / मे गृहं दिशानि । त्वां ... दर्शयानि ॥१६॥

17. Go away from me!

> मत्प्रव्रज । मद्व्रज ॥१७॥

18. He should be freed!

> मुच्यताम् ॥१८॥

19. I want to guard your wife.

> तव भार्यां रक्षाणि ॥१९॥

20. Die, enemy!

> शत्रो म्रियस्व ॥२०॥

21. Fight!

> युध्यध्वम् ॥२१॥

22. They (pl.) may command.

> आज्ञापयन्तु ॥२२॥

23. We want to dwell in your house.

> तव गृहे वसाम ॥२३॥

24. Thus it should happen!

> एवं भवतु । एवं वर्तताम् ॥२४॥

25. Strike the horse!

> अश्वं तुद ॥२५॥

26. They (pl.) should wander around.

> भ्रमन्तु । भ्राम्यन्तु ॥२६॥

27. Get intoxicated!

> माद्यत ॥२७॥

28. The horse should pull the load.

> अश्वो भारं कर्षतु ॥२८॥

29. We want to put the worlds in order.

> लोकान्कल्पयाम ॥२९॥

30. Drink the drink!

> पानं पिब ॥३०॥

31. Think!

> मन्यस्व ॥३१॥

32. They (pl.) should be conquered!

> जीयन्ताम् ॥३२॥

33. We want to be led by you.

> त्वया नीयामहै ॥३३॥

34. May it serve for happiness! (Blessing)

> सुखाय भवतात् ॥३४॥
