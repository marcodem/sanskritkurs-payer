# Exercise 45

A) Translate into Sanskrit (for past forms, please use the imperfect &lt;laṅ&gt;):

1. You split the (Buddhist) community (sangha).

> सङ्घं भिनत्सि ॥१॥

2. Did you (pl.) enjoy the earth?

> किं महीमभुङ्क्त ॥२॥

3. Did you receive a gift from the king?

> किं राज्ञो / राज्ञा दानमादत्थाः ॥३॥

4. Holy man, why do you not fear dangers?

> साधो कस्माद्भयेभ्यो न बिभेषi ॥४॥

5. Father, where are you going, leaving the family?

> पितः कुत्र गच्छन्कुलं जहासि ॥५॥

6. Śūdra woman, did you carry the load into the house of the wealthy Brahmin?

> शूद्रे ऽपि धनवतो / धनिनो ब्राह्मणस्य गृहं भारमबिभः ॥६॥

7. Goddess, you should fulfill the desire for happiness of the Vaiśya who has done meritorious deeds.

> देवि कृतपुण्यस्य वैश्यस्य सुखेष्टिं पिपूर्याः ॥७॥

8. Elephants, you should not break the bonds.

> हस्तिनो बन्धनानि न भञ्ज्यात । भिन्द्यात ॥८॥

9. O lion among men, why do you not direct your whole attention to the Absolute (Brahman)?

> नरसिंह कस्माद्ब्रह्मणि न समादधासि ॥९॥

10. Did you, O poet, measure the worlds?

> कवे कच्चिल्लोकानमिमीथाः ॥१०॥

B) Translate:

अर्जुन हतो वा प्राप्स्यसि स्वर्गं जित्वा वा भोक्ष्यसे महीं । तस्माद्भवता योध्यम् ॥१॥

> Arjuna, either killed you will reach heaven, or winning you will enjoy the earth. Therefore you must fight.

भवति कथमनन्तदुःखमरुणत् ॥२॥

> O venerable one, how did you stop the infinite suffering?

ब्राह्मण किमग्नौ घृतमजुहोः ॥३॥

> Brahmin, did you sacrifice ghee into the fire?

शत्रो शस्तेण जीवं न छिनत्सि ॥४॥

> Enemy! You do not cut the soul with a weapon.
