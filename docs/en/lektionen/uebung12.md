# Exercise 12

## Exercise

A) Form passive past sentences using the PPP (Past Passive Participle) from the active sentences of [Lesson 7](lektion07.md), Exercise A. For intransitive verbs and verbs of motion, use active past constructions with the PPP.

1. ब्राह्मणेन देव इष्टः ।
   The god was worshipped by the Brahmin.
2. गुरुणा फलं खादितम् ।
   The fruit was eaten by the teacher.
3. साधुः स्वर्गं गतः ।
   The holy man went to heaven.
4. शूद्रा नरकं गता ।
   The Śūdra woman went to hell.
5. शूद्रो जितः ॥
   The Śūdra was conquered.

B) Provide the corresponding PPPs for the verb forms from [Lesson 10](lektion10.md), Exercise A. Note that for a form like *sṛjati*, the PPP exists in all three genders (Masc., Fem., Neut.).

1. sṛjati (to create) -> सृष्टः (sṛṣṭaḥ)
2. yudhyate (to fight) -> युद्धः (yuddhaḥ)
3. bhavanti (to be) -> भूताः (bhūtāḥ)
4. yajati (to sacrifice) -> इष्टः (iṣṭaḥ)
5. nṛtyati (to dance) -> नृत्तः (nṛttaḥ)
6. nayanti (to lead) -> नीताः (nītāḥ)
7. smarati (to remember) -> स्मृतः (smṛtaḥ)
8. śṛṇvanti (to hear) -> श्रुताः (śrutaḥ)
9. paśyati (to see) -> दृष्टः (dṛṣṭaḥ) - from root *dṛś*
10. jayanti (to conquer) -> जिताः (jitāḥ)
