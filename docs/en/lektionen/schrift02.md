# Script Exercise 2

### Consonants (Vyañjana)

Devanāgarī consonants are organized scientifically according to their place of articulation.

#### 1. Velars (Kanthya)
Articulated at the throat.
![](/images/schrift003.jpg)

#### 2. Palatals (Tālavya)
Articulated at the hard palate.
![](/images/schrift004.jpg)

## Exercise

A) Practice writing the Velars: **ka, kha, ga, gha, ṅa**.

B) Practice writing the Palatals: **ca, cha, ja, jha, ña**.

C) Read and transliterate:
क ख ग घ ङ
च छ ज झ ञ

D) Write in Devanāgarī (using Virāma for vowel-less consonants):
k, kh, g, gh, ṅ
c, ch, j, jh, ñ
