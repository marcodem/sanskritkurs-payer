B) Translate:

प्रजहाति यदा कामानात्मन्येवात्मना तुष्टः स्थितप्रज्ञस्तदोच्यते ॥१॥

> When he gives up all desires and is satisfied in the self by the self, then he is called one of "firm insight" (sthitaprajña).

क्रोधाद्भवति संमोहः  
संमोहात्स्मृतिविभ्रमः ।  
स्मृतिभ्रंशाद्बुद्धिनाशो  
बुद्धिनाशात्प्रनश्यति ॥२॥

> From anger arises delusion; from delusion comes loss of memory; from loss of memory comes the destruction of insight; from the destruction of insight he perishes. (Bhagavad Gītā 2.63)

नास्ति बुद्धिरयुक्तस्य ॥३॥

> There is no insight for the uncontrolled (one who is not yoked/disciplined). (Bhagavad Gītā 2.66)
