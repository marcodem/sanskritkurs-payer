# Exercise 54

*First published here:* 2009-03-11

A) Translate the following forms in writing and form the corresponding aorist forms:

1.  यन्ति (they go) - अगुः
2.  पिबामि (I drink) - अपाम्
3.  ददौ (I have given / he has given) - अदाम् । अदात्
4.  बभूव (I / he was / you (pl.) were) - अभूवम् । अभूत् । अभूत
5.  दधति (they place) - अधुः
6.  ऐम (we went) - अगाम
7.  पपिथ (you have drunk / you have protected) - अपाः (only to *pā* 1 "drink")
8.  तिष्ठति (he stands) - अस्थात्
9.  इयेथ (you have gone) - अगाः
10.  पप (you (pl.) have drunk) - अपात
11.  एष्यथ (you (pl.) will go) - अगात
12.  तस्थुः (they stood) - अस्थुः
13.  अधत्त (you (pl.) placed) - अधात
14.  अददाः (you gave) - अदाः
15.  अभवन् (they were) - अभूवन्
16.  ददिम (we have given) - अदाम
17.  भिद्यते (it is split) - अभेदि
18.  उद्यते (it is said) - अवादि
19.  स्तूयते (he is praised) - अस्तावि
20.  कृष्यते (it is pulled / plowed) - अकर्षि
21.  जायते (passive: he is born) - अजनि
22.  गीयते (it is sung) - अगायि
23.  गम्यते (it is gone) - अगामि
