1. **śravaṇa** (n. hearing): *śravaṇāya* / *śravaṇebhyaḥ*

## Sentences & Compounds

Translate and analyze the compounds:

1. ब्राह्मणो देवप्रतिमादर्शनाय गर्भगृहं विशति ॥१॥
   The Brahmin enters the inner sanctuary (*garbha-gṛham*) to see the image of the god (*deva-pratimā-darśanāya*).
2. नरा धनलाभाय व्रतानि चरन्ति ॥२॥
   People perform vows (*vratāni*) for the sake of obtaining wealth (*dhana-lābhāya*).
3. गुरुर्धर्मोपदेशाय नगरं गतः ॥३॥
   The master has gone to the city for the purpose of teaching the Dharma (*dharmopadeśāya*).
4. स्वर्गेभ्यो नराः पुण्यं कर्तुमिच्छन्ति ॥६॥
   Men wish to perform meritorious deeds for the sake of the heavens (*svargebhyaḥ*).
5. मरणाय जना जायन्ते ॥९॥
   Beings are born in order to die (*maraṇāya*).

## Greetings and Expressions (Dative of Purpose/Greeting)

1. **namaḥ** (Salutation):
   * *Śivāya namaḥ* (Salutation to Shiva!)
   * *Śrī-gaṇeśāya namaḥ* (Salutation to Lord Ganesha!)
2. **svasti** (Well-being/Farewell):
   * *Svasti bhavate* (Well-being to you! / Goodbye!)
3. **svāgatam** (Welcome):
   * *Svāgatam bhavadbhyaḥ* (Welcome to you all!)
4. **punardarśanāya** (Until we see each other again / Goodbye!)
