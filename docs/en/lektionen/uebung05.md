# Exercise 5

### Grammar Review: Ablative, Genitive, and Locative Cases

## Exercise

A) Translate into English:
1. *Naraḥ kṣetrāt grāmaṃ gacchati.* = The man goes from the field to the village.
2. *Guruḥ śiṣyasya pustakaṃ paṭhati.* = The teacher reads the book of the student.
3. *Vane bahavaḥ gajāḥ vasanti.* = Many elephants live in the forest.

B) Translate into Sanskrit:
1. He comes from the city (*nagarāt*).
2. The house of the king (*nṛpasya gṛham*) is large.
3. There is water in the pot (*kumbhe jalam asti*).

C) Complete the declension of **nara** (all cases, Singular and Plural).
