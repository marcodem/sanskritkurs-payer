# Script Exercise 5

### Vowel Diacritics (Mātrās)

When vowels follow a consonant, they are written as diacritic marks attached to the consonant.

![](/images/schrift010.jpg)

![](/images/schrift011.jpg)

### Visarga and Anusvāra

- **Anusvāra** (ṃ): A dot above the line (ं) representing a nasal sound.
- **Visarga** (ḥ): Two dots after the consonant (ः) representing an unvoiced breath.

![](/images/schrift012.jpg)
