# Exercise 7

### Grammar Review: Pronominal Declension (tad, yad, kim)

## Exercise

A) Translate into English:
1. *Sa naraḥ śālāṃ gacchati.* = That man goes to the school.
2. *Yaḥ paṭhati sa jānāti.* = He who reads, he knows.
3. *Kaḥ tatra asti?* = Who matches [is] there? / Who is there?

B) Translate into Sanskrit:
1. Which boy (Nominative: *yaḥ bālaḥ*) speaks?
2. Looking at them (Accusative plural masculine: *tān paśyan*).
3. To whom (Dative singular masculine: *kasmai*) do you give the book?

C) Decline **tad** (that) in the masculine (Singular, Dual, Plural).
