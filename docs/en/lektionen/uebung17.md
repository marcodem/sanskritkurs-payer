# Exercise 17

## Exercise

A) Insert the correct verb form and translate the sentences:

1. ब्राह्मनो ऽनृतं न ... (brū / vac / vad) ।
   **ब्रवीति / वक्ति / वदति ।**
   A Brahmin does not speak untruth.
2. क्षत्रियो जनान् ... (pā / rakṣ) ।
   **पाति / रक्षति ।**
   A Kṣatriya protects the people.
3. बलवद्योधो द्विजारीन् ... (ji / han / yudh) ।
   **जयति / हन्ति / युध्यते ।**
   The powerful warrior conquers / kills / fights the enemies of the Twice-born.
4. ब्राह्मणकविर्लोकेश्वरम् ... (stu / yaj) ।
   **स्तौति / यजति ।**
   The Brahmin poet praises the Lord of the World.
5. अग्निर्यज्ञान्नम् ... (ad / dah) ।
   **अत्ति / दहति ।**
   The fire consumes / burns the sacrificial food.
6. बालवैश्यो धेनुम् ... (duh / rakṣ / pā) ।
   **दोग्धि / रक्षति / पाति ।**
   The young Vaiśya milks / protects the cow.

B) Pluralize the agent and the verb in the sentences from A):

1. ब्राह्मणा अनृतं न **ब्रुवन्ति / वदन्ति** ।
   Brahmins do not speak untruth.
2. क्षत्रिया जनान् **पान्ति / रक्षन्ति** ।
   Kṣatriyas protect the people.

## Verb Review: Root and Meaning

Translate and identify the root of the following verb forms:

1. **adanti** (अद् 2P): they eat
2. **santi** (अस् 2P): they are
3. **yanti** (इ 2P): they go
4. **icchatī** (इष् 6P): he/she wishes
5. **tanoti** (तन् 8U): he stretches / performs
6. **dogdhi** (दुह् 2U): he milks
7. **pṛcchati** (प्रच्छ् 6P): he asks
8. **bhavanti** (भू 1P): they become / are
9. **mriyante** (मृ 4Ā): they die
10. **rodi ti** (रुद् 2P): he/she cries
11. **stauti** (स्तु 2U): he praises
12. **hanti** (हन् 2P): he kills / strikes
