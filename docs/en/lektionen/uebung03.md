# Exercise 3

### Grammar Review: A-stems (Masculine and Neuter)

## Exercise

A) Transliterate and translate:
1. नरः फलं खादति | (*Naraḥ phalaṃ khādati.*) = The man eats a fruit.
2. गजा वनं गच्छन्ति | (*Gajā vanaṃ gacchanti.*) = The elephants go to the forest.
3. शिष्या गुरुं स्मरन्ति | (*Śiṣyā guruṃ smaranti.*) = The students remember the teacher.

B) Write in Devanāgarī and translate:
1. *Bālā vanaṃ paśyanti.* = (बाला वनं पश्यन्ति |) = The girls see the forest.
2. *Guruḥ śiṣyaṃ pṛcchati.* = (गुरुः शिष्यं पृच्छति |) = The teacher asks the student.

C) Decline **nara** (man) and **phala** (fruit) as follows:
*   Nominative Singular & Plural
*   Accusative Singular & Plural
