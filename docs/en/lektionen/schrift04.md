## Exercise

A) Practice writing the Labials: **pa, pha, ba, bha, ma**.

B) Practice writing the Semivowels: **ya, ra, la, va**.

C) Practice writing the Sibilants and the Aspirate: **śa, ṣa, sa, ha**.

D) Read and transliterate:
प फ ब भ म
य र ल व
श ष स ह

E) Note the difference between **ba** (ब) and **va** (व).
