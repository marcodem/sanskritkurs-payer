# Exercise 6

### Grammar Review: Feminine Ā-stems

## Exercise

A) Translate into English:
1. *Bālā śālāṃ gacchati.* = The girl goes to the school.
2. *Kanyāyoḥ mātā vadati.* = The mother of the two girls speaks.
3. *Devatāḥ rātrau na dṛśyante.* = The deities are not seen at night.

B) Translate into Sanskrit:
1. The student (fem.) reads the book. (*Śiṣyā pustakaṃ paṭhati.*)
2. We see the goddesses. (*Devīḥ paśyāmaḥ.* - actually using *devatāḥ* for ā-stems: *Devatāḥ paśyāmaḥ.*)
3. She sits in the house. (*Sā gṛhe sīdati.*)

C) Decline **kanyā** (girl) in all cases (Singular, Dual, Plural).
