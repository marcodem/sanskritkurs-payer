### 45.1.2. Class 7 (*rudhādi*)

**Rule**: When the root-final *k* or *g* of a Class 7 verb stands between the nasal *ṅ* and a dental of the ending, it may optionally be dropped (e.g., *yuṅkte* or *yuṅte*).

## 45.2. The Vocative Singular (*āmantritam*)

Most singular vocative forms are characterized by the **stem ending in a high-grade (*guṇa*) vowel** or by the **pure stem**.

**Common Vocative Singular Forms:**
| Declension Pattern | Vocative Singular | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Masculine in -a** | *deva* | "O God" |
| **Neuter in -a** | *phala* | "O Fruit" |
| **Feminine in -ā** | **devate** | "O Deity" |
| **Masculine in -i** | *kave* | "O Poet" |
| **Feminine in -i** | *mate* | "O Thought" |
| **Masculine in -u** | *śatro* | "O Enemy" |
| **Feminine in -u** | *dheno* | "O Cow" |
| **Feminine in -ī** | **devi** | "O Goddess" |
| **Masculine in -ṛ** | *pitar* | "O Father" |
| **Feminine in -ṛ** | *mātar* | "O Mother" |
| **Masculine in -an** | *rājan* | "O King" |
| **Neuter in -an** | *nāma / nāman* | "O Name" |
| **Masculine in -man/-van** | *ātman* | "O Self" |
| **Masculine in -in** | *balin* | "O Mighty One" |
| **Masculine in -ant** | *balin / bali* | |
| **mahānt** m. | *mahan* | "O Great One" |
| **mahānt** n. | *mahat* | (Identical to Nom.) |
| **Masculine in -mant/-vant** | *guṇavan* | "O Virtuous One" |
| **Neuter in -mant/-vant** | *guṇavat* | (Identical to Nom.) |

## 45.4. Exercise

A) Translate into Sanskrit (use the Imperfect *laṅ* for past tenses):
1.  You are splitting the (Buddhist) community (*saṅghabheda*).
2.  Did you all enjoy the earth?
3.  Did you receive a gift from the king?
4.  Holy man, why do you not fear dangers?
5.  Father, where are you leaving the family to?
6.  O Shudra woman, did you carry the burden into the house of the wealthy Brahmin?
7.  Goddess, you should fulfill the desire for happiness of the Vaishya who has done meritorious deeds.
8.  Elephants, you should not break the fetters.
9.  O lion-like man, why do you not direct your whole attention to the Absolute (Brahman)?
10. Poet, have you measured the worlds?

B) Translate:
1.  *arjuna hato vā prāpsyasi svargaṃ jitvā vā bhokṣyase mahiṃ | tasmād bhavatā yodhyam || 1 ||*
2.  *bhavati katham anantaduḥkham aruṇat || 2 ||*
3.  *brāhmaṇa kim agnau ghṛtam ajuhos || 3 ||*
4.  *śatro śasteṇa jīvaṃ na chinatsi || 4 ||*
