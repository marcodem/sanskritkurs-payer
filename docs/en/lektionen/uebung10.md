# Exercise 10

### Grammar Review: Imperfect Tense and Augments

## Exercise

A) Translate into English:
1. *Bālāḥ apaṭhan.* = The children were reading / read (Imperfect).
2. *Gajaḥ vanaṃ agacchat.* = The elephant went to the forest.
3. *Ahaṃ guruṃ apṛccham.* = I asked the teacher.

B) Translate into Sanskrit (using the Imperfect):
1. The king spoke (*nṛpaḥ avadat*).
2. You saw the forest (*tvaṃ vanaṃ apaśyaḥ*).
3. We rejoiced (*vayaṃ amodāmahi*).

C) Change from Present to Imperfect:
*   *paṭhati* » *apaṭhat*
*   *gacchanti* » *agacchan*
*   *vadāmaḥ* » *avadāma*
