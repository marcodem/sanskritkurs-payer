# Exercise 26

## Exercise: Future Tense Formations

Form the future tense (*-sya-* / *-iṣya-*) based on the present tense form provided.

### A) Future without connecting vowel '-i-'
1. **atti** -> *atsyati* (he will eat)
2. **prāpnuvanti** -> *prāpsyanti* (they will reach)
3. **sam-yanti** -> *sameṣyanti* (they will come together)
4. **vi-jayante** -> *vijeṣyante* (they will conquer)
5. **apa-nayan tī** -> *apaneṣyanti* (they will lead away)
6. **pacati** -> *pakṣyati* (he will cook)
7. **piva-ti** (root *pā*) -> *pāsyati* (he will drink)
8. **pṛcchati** -> *prakṣyati* (he will ask)
9. **muñcati** -> *mokṣyati* (he will release)
10. **yajaté** -> *yakṣyate* (he will sacrifice for himself)
11. **śaknoti** -> *śakṣyati* (he will be able)
12. **śṛṇoti** -> *śroṣyati* (he will hear)
13. **tiṣṭhati** (root *sthā*) -> *sthāsyati* (he will stand)

### B) Future with connecting vowel '-i-'
1. **asyati** -> *asiṣyati* (he will throw)
2. **āsate** -> *āsiṣyante* (they will sit)
3. **icchati** (root *iṣ*) -> *eṣiṣyati* (he will wish)
4. **khādati** -> *khādiṣyati* (he will eat)
5. **carati** -> *cariṣyati* (he will wander/perform)
6. **jāyante** (root *jan*) -> *janiṣyante* (they will be born)
7. **patanti** -> *patiṣyanti* (they will fall)
8. **roditi** -> *rodiṣyati* (he/she will cry)
9. **ghnanti** (root *han*) -> *haniṣyanti* (they will kill/strike)
10. **smarati** -> *smariṣyati* (he will remember)
